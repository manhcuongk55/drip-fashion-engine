## The Pitch

> **SHEIN wins by selling clothes fast. DRIP wins by selling trends first.**

---

## 1. Problem

The fashion industry is broken in two opposing ways:

| Fast Fashion (SHEIN) | Slow Fashion (Everyone Else) |
|---|---|
| Trend → shelf in 7 days | Trend → shelf in 90–180 days |
| Zero local identity | Can't compete on speed |
| Massive overproduction | Miss the trend window entirely |
| Low quality, low trust | By the time they ship, it's dead |

**Vietnam has 200+ apparel factories and a booming creator economy — but no platform that connects them to real-time trends.**

---

## 2. Solution

**DRIP** is an AI-powered fashion intelligence platform that detects viral trends from short video platforms and converts them into production-ready drops through Vietnam's local factory network — in **48 hours**.

```
TikTok / Instagram  →  AI Trend Radar  →  Design Studio
                                               ↓
                    Orders  ←  Factory Network  ←  Brief
                       ↓
               Creator Feed  →  Buyers
```

---

## 3. Product — 6 Core Modules

### 🎯 Trend Radar
- AI scans TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest every 60 seconds
- Scores each trend: velocity, engagement, demographic fit
- Surfaces top 2,000–10,000 signals per day

### ✨ Design Studio
- Paste a video URL → AI extracts look, fabric, silhouette
- Generates a production-ready design brief instantly
- AI confidence score, color palette, size recommendations

### 🏭 Factory Network
- 127 verified Vietnam factories (HCMC, Hanoi, Da Nang, Hue...)
- Real-time capacity, lead times, and specializations
- Micro-batch minimum: 50 pieces per drop

### 📦 Order Tracker
- Live production tracking from design approval → QC → ship
- Factory cam feed, QC reports, priority notifications

### 🌟 Creator Feed
- Influencers drop directly to followers
- Live countdown, stock bar, real-time buy flow
- TikTok Shop-style social commerce

### 📊 Analytics
- Revenue, sell-through rate, AI prediction accuracy
- Traffic source breakdown: TikTok dominates at 54%
- Category performance by style and creator
